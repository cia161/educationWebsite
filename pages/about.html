My Story

I'm Fatima, and I am excited to announce my candidacy for the Champaign Unit 4 School District Board of Education. I'm running because I believe in coming together to serve every student in our community. As a biostatistician, a proud Illinois graduate, and a parent of three children who have attended Unit 4 schools, I bring professional expertise and personal experience to this role. Fun fact: the same teacher at Bottenfield who taught my husband later taught our eldest in kindergarten! I am passionate about creating a school board that represents all voices. My goal is to foster collaboration and respect among board members to make the best decisions for our students, families, and educators.
