How to Vote

  • Online voter registration is currently closed, but you can still register and vote in person at a polling location until 7 PM on Election Day.
  • Early voting locations are listed on the Champaign County Clerk’s website.
  • Request a mail-in ballot here.

For more information, read more on the Vote Champaign website.

Why Your Vote Matters

  • Education Quality: School board members make critical decisions about curriculum, funding, and policies that shape students’ educational experiences.
  • Resource Allocation: The board oversees the district’s budget, ensuring resources benefit students and staff effectively.
  • Community Representation: A diverse and representative school board ensures that all voices in the community are heard.
